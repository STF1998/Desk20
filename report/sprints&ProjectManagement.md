## Group Working Methods

### Overview

When developing a single page application, it is critical to consider development methodology, particularly given the large number of moving parts associated with full-stack development. When discussing the development stage, we discussed the pros and cons of an agile vs waterfall methodology before agreeing on the agile approach. The waterfall approach is focused more on a rigid structure of tasks that lead to a final product whereas an agile approach prioritises a more flexible and iterative process to software development.
